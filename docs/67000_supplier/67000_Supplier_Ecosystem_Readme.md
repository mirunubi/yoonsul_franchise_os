# 1 Purpose

< Purpose >

The supplier ecosystem lane anchors the external sourcing and vendor boundary for Phase 3.

This lane exists to:

- define supplier and vendor ownership boundaries.
- keep supplier relationships separate from internal store, HQ, and customer operations.
- provide the anchor for future supplier subsystem docs.

# 2 What Belongs Here

< In Scope >

- supplier relationships.
- vendor coordination.
- sourcing continuity.
- external ecosystem boundaries for production and procurement.

# 3 What Does Not Belong Here

< Out Of Scope >

- store execution or frontline capture.
- HQ policy and rollout governance.
- customer CRM or membership lifecycle ownership.
- deep constitutional rules that belong in `3rd_00018`.
